About

My Story

Hello, I'm Tetyana Dixon, an upcoming UX designer with a diverse background in real estate and marketing. My cross-cultural perspective, bilingual proficiency in Ukrainian and Russian, and passion for creating user-centered designs drive my approach to crafting intuitive and impactful digital experiences. Transitioning into UX design has allowed me to leverage my skills in understanding user needs and translating them into seamless, engaging interfaces.

My career journey has been a tapestry of experiences in real estate, hospitality, and marketing, each providing unique insights into understanding and anticipating user needs. This diverse background has honed my ability to empathize with users from various walks of life and craft solutions that cater to their specific requirements. My interests in meditation, reading, and nature not only offer me relaxation but also inspire my creative process, allowing me to approach design challenges with a clear and focused mind.
